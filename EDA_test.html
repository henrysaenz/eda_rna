📌 Explanation of the Variables in adata_test.obs#

Column

Description

batch_indices

Numerical index of the experimental batch the cell belongs to. Helps identify samples from different experiments.

n_genes

Number of detected genes in each cell. Indicates gene expression diversity.

percent_mito

Percentage of mitochondrial genes in the cell. A high value may indicate cellular stress or dead cells.

leiden_subclusters

Cluster assigned to the cell using the Leiden algorithm, which detects subpopulations within the data.

cell_types

The assigned cell type, such as NKT (Natural Killer T cells), CD8 T cells, B cells, etc.

tissue

The tissue the cell comes from, such as Spleen or Lymph Node.

batch

Identifier of the original experimental batch, e.g., SLN111-D1. It may represent samples from different experiments or patients.
